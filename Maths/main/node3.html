Boundary and Initial Conditions

The general solution to a 1st Order DE always contains one undefined constant of integration, like . A boundary (for x) or initial (for t) condition is given, typcially y(x) at a given x value.

Example: T goes from 90c to 70c in 10 minutes(t), the room temperature is 20c(T), find T after 20 minutes.
